Evaluators, regardless of their skill and experience, can only emulate users—and not necessarily typical users of the product. Feedback from target users can add an important dimension to some expert evaluations—for example, of alternative navigation approaches for an existing product, or of a product recently targeted to a new user audience. In these cases, TecEd conducts a few usability walkthroughs with people whose characteristics are similar to the target user audience to supplement the evaluation. When evaluating products in specific technical domains, a domain specialist joins the usability specialists on the evaluation team.
